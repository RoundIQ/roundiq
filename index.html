FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot

Does it have GPS distance to the pin?+
Not yet — GPS distance is on our roadmap for a future version. Right now Good Day Golf focuses on round tracking, handicap calculation, and stat analysis using scorecard data.
What does "no subscription ever" mean?+
You get 14 days free to try everything. After that, you pay $24.99 once and own the app forever. No monthly fee, no premium tier, no features locked behind a paywall.
Does it work on iPhone and Android?+
Yes — Good Day Golf works on any phone or tablet. Add it to your home screen on iPhone (Safari) or Android (Chrome) and it looks and feels like a native app.
How is the handicap calculated?+
We use the World Handicap System (WHS) — the same method used by golf associations worldwide. Your handicap updates automatically after every round.
What if my course isn't in the database?+
You can enter course details manually — pars and yardages hole by hole. Save it once and it's always available.
How does the AI caddie work?+
Before each hole we send your hole details and personal stats to Anthropic's Claude AI, which generates a personalized tip based on how you typically play.
